ABOUT US


Who We Are ?

We collaborate with our esteemed clients to create memorable visual content for a variety of platforms and mediums, from concept to completion.


Unmatched Bespoke Content

BACKSTAGE is a leading visual content creation company (photography and video). founded in 2018, backstage has an award winning grounding in high-fashion photography. innovation has always been in our dna. backstage was the first visual content company to specialize in e-commerce.

backstage brings unmatched experience in implementing and scaling visual content solutions. this has made us the premier visual content partner for a number of respected retailers, luxury brands, and fashion houses.
